FROM A FELON TO A
MULTI-MILLIONAIRE TRADER

From SWAT banging at my door to seven figures in my account

2020, CALIFORNIA

On The Run In California

In 2020, I wasnt vacationing in California I was hiding.

The feds were circling. My name was being whispered in
investigations. My friends were getting picked off one by one. But instead of running, I risked it all and decided to double down on trading.

I rented a small spot with no furniturejust a mattress, my desk, and a trading setup. 

That day, that room became my war room.

It was genuinely life or death.

I was making big wins but even bigger losses. I had no plan. I was gambling with size I couldnt justify, trying to out-trade my demons.

And I lied to myself, saying:

This next big flip, and Im out.

But deep down, I wasnt trading to escapeI was addicted to the gambling. Which caused havoc:

SWAT STORMING IN, GUNS DRAWN.

Reality Came Crashing Down

My life was a high-wire act with no safety net. Each morning, I'd wake up checking my accounts, riding the volatile waves of digital currencies, chasing the next rush without strategy or discipline.

I was just another desperate hustler thinking I could outsmart the system.

In 2020, I was hiding in California. Not vacationingrunning. The feds were circling while I recklessly gambled with crypto. Big wins, bigger losses.

2022: PROP FIRMS

MY Biggest Breakthrough

Id lost everythingagain. I had no trading account, no house, no income.

I was delivering DoorDash, scraping together every dollar just to fund my $100 trading account.

I knew that it was over, but I couldnt accept it.

Thats when something changed, and I discovered the one most important thing that saved my life:

Prop Firms.

I learned that for $500, I could access $100,000 in capital. 

This cant be real. No way. 

But, does it even matter if it isnt? Its not like I really have a choice anyway.

Its either this or I go back. And I wont go back.

I failed the first challenge. Then the second. But I didnt stop.

Every morning, I trained like it was life or death.

5:30 AM. Cardio. Cold plunges. Journaling. Charting.

I rebuilt myselfmentally, physically, and emotionally.

No more gambling.

No more hoping.

Just calculated execution and accountability.

By the end of the year, Id built consistency. Not luck.

I was finally making money the right waylegally, ethically, and publicly.

*Phewthen it came*

Finally, my first feeling of relief.

2024

Breaking Free

June 27th.

I did it.

beat the case.

After three years of hell, 20+ court dates, and hiring a defense team I couldnt afford but couldnt live withoutI walked out free.

Not just from prison, but from the version of myself that kept sabotaging everything I touched.

Id lost 50 lbs. Quit weed. Built a mentorship & started trading live every day.

And most importantly, I stopped trying to get rich quick and started trading like a professional.

Thats when everything changed:

2025

Living The Dream

3 years after the worst day of my life, I did a complete 180.

I bought my dream home. Surprised my wife with a brand-new BMW.

Retired my parents. Bought my childhood house back that we lost in the crash of 2008.

But none of that compares to what Im doing now

*Helping others use trading as a way out*

But it isnt just about trading. Its about transformation.

Because if I made it from that to this?

Then you can go from where youre at today to where you want to be.

No excuses.
